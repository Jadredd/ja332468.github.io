Welcome to Central Florida Bone & Joint Institute

Central Florida Bone & Joint Institute offers you the very best in orthopaedic care. All of our surgeries are performed at top rated local hospitals and centers that provide their patients with the same quality of care we strive for here at Central Florida Bone & Joint Institute. Our doctors perform inpatient surgery at Florida Hospital FISH in Orange City. At FISH, there is an orthopaedic specific floor with dedicated staff, providing you attentive, personal care. All outpatient surgeries are done at the beautiful Orange City Surgery Center.
